Detailed Description

bbMedia stores all of the graphics, etc needed to display everything drawn to the screen, pertaining to one of the game's maps media is reloaded every time a new map is loaded or made current. we can loosen our restriction on floating point numbers out of a sense of necessity a bbMap should be update-able without having it's bbMedia loaded;
